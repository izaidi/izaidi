How do I configure WebMynd?
You can access your WebMynd settings by selecting "WebMynd Settings" in your Firefox "Tools" menu.
 
 
How do I remove WebMynd?
To remove the WebMynd extension, click "Tools" in your Firefox menu and select "Add-ons." In the window that appears, select WebMynd and click the "Uninstall" button. You'll need to restart Firefox to complete the uninstall process.

WebMynd also stores thumbnails and snapshots of recorded websites in a directory called "WebMyndFiles" in your Firefox profile directory. You can delete this directory to completely remove all traces of your WebMynd history.
