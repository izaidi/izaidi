What is the WebMynd search sidebar?
WebMynd presents personalized, configurable search results on the right-hand side of the Google results page.
 
 
How do I add and remove search tools?
You can use the "Sites" menu to add search tools to the WebMynd sidebar. To remove a search tool, click the "x" to the right of the section title. You can also click "Configure" to switch to a special panel for adding, removing and ranking your search tools.
 
 
How do I change the order of search tools?
In both the main panel and the configure panel, you can drag and drop the tools in your sidebar to change their order. Just hold down your left mouse button over the title of a section and move it up or down.
 
 
How do I see more results?
You can scroll down through all the results in each section, and if you click on the title of the section another tab will open showing you all the available results.
 
 
Where can I get more help?
You can check our status blog and our Twitter for service updates. If you do not find what you need here, please do contact us directly.
