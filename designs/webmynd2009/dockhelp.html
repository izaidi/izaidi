What is the WebMynd Dock?
The WebMynd Dock improves your browsing experience by making it easy to return to tabs or pages you've viewed recently, and lets you share links with your friends on sites like Facebook and Twitter.
 
 
How do I enable and disable the Dock?
The Dock can be disabled in your WebMynd settings. You can access your settings by selecting "WebMynd Settings" in your Firefox "Tools" menu. You can also disable the Dock by opening it and clicking the "Disable" link.
 
 
How do I open and close the Dock?
When the Dock is enabled, use the bar on the left hand side of your browser window to open and close it.
 
 
What is the "Recently viewed" list?
This is a list of the pages you've viewed most recently, either by browsing or by switching tabs. Click on a title to return to that page. If the page is already open in another tab, Firefox will just switch to that tab.

If you have recording enabled, you'll see a thumbnail of each page as you mouse over it.
 
 
How do I email a link to multiple people?
In the box for your friend's email address, you can enter multiple addresses, separated by spaces or commas, and your link will be sent to each of them with the message you provide.
